💖 About This Club

Our goal: to read romance-focused stories where the love story always runs alongside the main plot.

Genres we love:

  • 🖤 Dark Romance
  • ✨ Romantasy
  • 🏒 Sports Romance
  • 💌 Emotional Contemporary Romance
  • 💼 Billionaire / Power Romance
  • 🔥 Mafia Romance
  • 🌍 Small Town Romance
  • 🎓 College / New Adult Romance
  • 🕰️ Historical Romance

We select a romance sub-genre each month, keeping our reading experience fresh while exploring different love stories.

Every book features an FMC + MMC and delivers emotional tension, unforgettable fictional men, and stories that make us spiral together.

We try to meet 3rd Saturday of every month at 11:30 AM for brunch.

Next sub-genre is 🕰️ Historical Romance

Everyone will bring a book of their choice from the chosen sub-genre, we then vote on it during the meet-up to select the Next Read
